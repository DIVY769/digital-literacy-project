## Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Created a one-page visual infographic using Canva covering what digital literacy means, safe internet practices, and professional online presence. The infographic is designed for first-year students who are new to understanding responsible digital behavior.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Each platform serves a distinct purpose in building a credible online identity for academic and career growth over the next four years.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner coding challenge on HackerRank and created a 5-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms to help batchmates test their knowledge.

### Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette
Drafted two professional emails following proper etiquette guidelines. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist for responsible digital communication as a college student.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Researched UPI/Online Payment Fraud, wrote a detailed case study on how such scams operate, and created a prevention checklist with 8+ actionable safety tips specific to Indian college students.
